Welcome to Dice Dungeon

Embark on an adventurous journey through dungeons filled with dangers and treasures. Use your wits and dice to navigate through challenging scenarios and emerge victorious!

Game Setup

To play Dice Dungeon, you will need:

  • A set of standard six-sided dice (d6).
  • Each player will need one d6 dice and one four-sided dice (d4).
  • Dungeon tiles or a way to represent the dungeon layout.

Game Rules

When to Draw a Card?

  • Draw a card whenever you open a door.
  • Follow instructions on the card. If no door placement is specified, roll a 1d6: even - one door, odd - two doors.

Enemy Tiles:

  • Enemies are represented by numbers in the grid within the app.
  • Place a 1d6 down with that number, representing their health.

Enemy Actions:

  • Enemies perform one attack and one movement action per turn, unless stated otherwise in the app.
  • Enemies roll a 1d6 for movement and a 1d4 for attack.
  • Enemies target the closest player or the last one who damaged them.

Player Turns and Actions:

  • Each player can perform two actions per turn: Move, Melee Attack, Ranged Attack, Heal.

Health and Lives:

  • Each player starts with 3 lives and a maximum health of 6.

Winning the Game:

Complete the mission objectives while keeping all players alive through the end of the scenario.

Tips and Strategies:

  • Work as a team and balance your strategies between attack and defense.
